About The Dream

Built for slow mornings, chai at sunset, and the soul of the northern valleys.

This concept brings together the warmth of a restaurant, the charm of a coffee cafe, the freshness of a flower counter, and the calm of a reading nook. Guests can relax in a space inspired by Gilgit and Hunza, with earthy textures, scenic views, and a peaceful atmosphere.

The design direction blends alpine calm with soft traditional warmth. Pine greens, walnut browns, apricot cream tones, and muted terracotta accents create a look that feels elegant, grounded, and memorable.
